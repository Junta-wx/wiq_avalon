    HOW TO PLAY

    Goal: Good wins after 3 successful quests. Evil wins after 3 failed quests or if they assassinate Merlin.

    Roles:

    • Merlin: Knows the evil players (except Mordred).
    • Percival: Knows Merlin and Morgana (as they appear to him).
    • Assassin: If Good wins, he gets one chance to pick Merlin and win for Evil.
    • Morgana: Appears as Merlin to Percival.

    Gameplay:

    1. The Leader proposes a team for a Quest.
    2. Everyone Votes to Approve or Reject the team.
    3. If approved, the team members secretly vote to Success or Fail.
